My City: Patna

The Historic and Cultural Capital of Bihar

Introduction

Patna is the capital city of Bihar, located on the southern bank of the River Ganga. It is one of the oldest continuously inhabited places in the world, with a history dating back to ancient times.

Historical Significance

Ancient Patna, known as Pataliputra, was the seat of power for several mighty empires such as the Maurya and Gupta dynasties. It was a center of learning and governance, with scholars like Chanakya and rulers like Ashoka having walked its soil.

Modern Patna

Today, Patna is a growing urban hub with modern amenities, educational institutions, and infrastructural development. It serves as a major political, cultural, and educational center of Eastern India.

My Connection

I am proud to belong to this historic city. From its bustling streets to its peaceful ghats, Patna blends heritage with modern living, offering a unique identity to its residents.
